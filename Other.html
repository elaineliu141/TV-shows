Other Great Shows

People in the US watch TV for the same reason everybody else watches TV: It's fun, not exhausting, but sufficiently distracting to take us away from the drudgery and stress of our lives. Today is the GOLDEN AGE of TV. Never has there been so many amazing things to watch and with such variety. Here are some other good shows to watch! P.S. Only watch these shows after you finish Game of Thrones!

After young man Atsushi Nakajima saves a man from his wish of suicide, he and his friends join the "Arms Detective Company," a league of literary figures that also harness special powers.

Subaru Natsuki is an ordinary student who is lost in an alternate world, where he is rescued by a silver-haired girl. He then finds out he has the power to rewind death, back to the time he first came to this world.

Yuri on Ice follows Katsuki Yuri as he returns to Japan after a competition. Unexpectedly, high-level skater Viktor begins coaching Yuri allowing him to take on the challenge of the Grand Prix series.

Friends

This TV show introduces the six main characters: Rachel Green, Monica Geller, Phoebe Buffay, Joey Tribbiani, Chandler Bing and Ross Geller. The episodic nature of the show sees the other characters having multiple dates, many of which go wrong (Monica dates a minor in one episode). The recurring character of Janice is introduced as a girlfriend Chandler breaks up with in an early episode, but frequently returns to through the ensuing ten seasons.

Big Bang Theory

The Big Bang Theory is a comedy series about four young scientists who know all about the world of physics, and one girl, who gives the physics world a real spin. Leonard Hofstadter, Sheldon Cooper, Howard Wolowitz and Rajesh Koothrappali are one of the world's smartest men with an intellectual capacity through the roof and a language with scientific words normal people only have one comment to ('What-). Finally, there's Penny, the gorgeous girl next-door to Leonard and Sheldon's apartment, and though she does not have any knowledge in physics or science, she makes success by being a funny character frequently having hilarious comments and on- and off-going relationships.

Cartoons

"Over the Garden Wall" is the story of two brothers, Wirt and Greg, who find themselves lost in the Unknown; a strange forest adrift in time. Wirt and Greg must travel across this strange landto find their way home. Join them as they encounter surprises and obstacles on their journey through the wood.

"Gravity Falls" is a fresh new comedy series that follows city kids and twins Dipper and Mabel Pines who are sent to spend the summer with their Great Uncle Stan who runs a tourist trap called The Mystery Shack in a remote northwestern town where nothing is what it seems.

The Amazing World Of Gumball follows the misadventures of Gumball, a twelve year old cat. With the help of his best friend Darwin, he gets into all sorts of antics. He is joined by his sister, Anais, a four year old bunny with heightened intelligence.

This show follows two groundskeepers, a six-foot-tall blue jay named Mordecai and Rigby the raccoon. It shows their adventures as they try to entertain themselves on a daily basis, while working on the park.

A boy named Finn and a wise dog, Jake, encounter multiple wacky adventures in the land of Ooo in a post-apocalypse world after the Great Mushroom War. With the help pf kings and queens, they defeat obstacles on the way.
