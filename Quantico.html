Quantico

The new recruits have just arrived at Quantico Base in Virginia and Special Agent Liam O'Connor tells them that the FBI Academy is the toughest boot camp and hardest grad school rolled into one. The agent hopefuls include Bay Area beauty Alex Weaver, Brooklyn hipster Simon Asher, clean cut Mormon Eric Packer, golden boy Caleb Haas, southern belle Shelby Wyatt, hijab wearing Nimah Anwar, and good looking Ryan Booth who manages to seduce Alex before classes begin. Some come from FBI families, others have been victims of terrorism.

Whatever their reason for joining, these recruits only have a 50/50 chance of finishing and there are no second chances. Between target practice and tests of physical endurance, they have to learn the art of investigation. Their first exercise is to find secrets about their fellow trainees. This task proves to be very enlightening- exposing more than they would like. But it pushes one of the recruits tragically too far.

This tragedy will reflect poorly on supervisor Liam and his friend and boss, Miranda Shaw, the director of Quantico. But they're about to face something even bigger -a recruit is suspected of masterminding the biggest attack on New York City since 9/11.

Actors

Alex Parrish

Alex was introduced as an FBI recruit at the FBI Academy, Quantico, Virginia. Considered to be the brightest recruit of her class, she eventually became an FBI field agent and later the prime suspect for a terrorist attack on Grand Central Terminal.

Ryan Booth

Ryan Booth is an ex-Marine and a former NAT at Quantico. He had believed he was working undercover as an FBI recruit, but Liam O'Connor revealed to him that he had been dropped as a special agent.

Nimah Amin

Nimah Amin, in the present day, was a former NAT at Quantico. Unlike her sister, she does not wear the hijab. After the graduation ceremony in Yes, she becomes an FBI agent.

Shelby Wyatt

Shelby Wyatt was a NAT, at the Academy where she had a friendship with her roommate Alex Parrish. In the flash-forward scenes, she is still in the FBI, but no longer a recruit.

Miranda Shaw

Miranda Shaw, in the Present Day, is the Assistant Director at the FBI Academy in Quantico. In the flash forwards, she is replaced as the AD by Liam O'Connor.

Liam O'Connor

Liam O'Connor, in the present day, was a Special Agent training new recruits at the FBI Academy in Quantico. In the flash-forward scenes 9 months in the future, he was the Assistant Director, having replaced Miranda Shaw.

Caleb Haas

Caleb Haas, in the present day, started off as a NAT, but after failing all of their tests on his first and second day, he becomes an analyst.

Simon Asher

Simon Asher was a NAT at the FBI Academy. After being kicked out of Quantico, he worked as an IT tech. He was one of the few people that believed in Alex's innocence.

Natalie Vasquez

Natalie Vasquez was a former top recruit at Quantico. Natalie was a trainee who had a rivalry with Alex. She graduated from the academy and became an FBI agent.
